=7th Century
==610s [Oct 2016 - July 2017]
===618
====July
 - Turso Theodorias (later surname Heleonas is adopted) is born.
==630s [June 2018 - Mar 2019]
===638
====June
 - 14th: Iskandro Anorias is killed in battle against the Hasamantes. His betrothal to Nicene is reorganized towards his younger brother Isaakios, who succeeds him as Count of Akros.
====July
 - The Imperial Kiprian campaign fails after the Siege of Naviopolis is broken; hostilities end. The Imperium gains the islands of Ramonas and Ioulas but fails to meet is primary objective of annexing Kipria.
====September
 - Exarch of Agion, Andro Angelidou, enters open revolt against the reigning emperor Mastesian II Amicianus, raises an army of professionals and levies totaling to around 18,000, and heads north, beginning the Agionic Revolt.
===639
====March
 - 7th: Battle of Mostos - An Agionic army routs a smaller Imperial force dispatched to put down Angelidou's revolt.
 - 13th: Battle of Lacea - The same Agionic force lifts an Imperial siege on the small trading city of Lacea, which had also entered into revolt. The destruction of two Imperial formations in such little time inspires the leadership of the city of Vanaea to secede from the Imperium as well. 
 - 29th: Siege of Soliaron - Agionic forces begin to lay siege onto the Imperial fortress of Soliaron, the last loyalist foothold south of the Herulene Plain.
====April
 - 5th: Exarch Andro Angelidou is killed besieging Soliaron. Left with little choice, the Agionic leadership chooses to elect a new Exarch whose bid to support and a young marshal Turso (then aged 19)
==640s [Apr 2019 - Jan 2020]
===640
====September
 - The Principality of Aphyrea revokes its status of allegiance to the Imperium, eventually directly allying itself with Agion.
===641
====January
 - The first Great Bay Council is held in Kelos, hosting diplomats and leaders from Agion, Aphyrea, Mevoeon, Lacea, and Vanaea, resolving to form a permanent strategic alliance between the five signatories. This alliance later becomes the basis for the League of Kelos.
====February
 - 5th: A son, Athaion, is born out of wedlock to Exarch Turso, who is campaigning across the Messene plain.
===642
====January
 - 10th: A summit between dissenting parties in the Imperium and the Agionic leadership meet.
====February
 - 8th: Velia, the second daughter of Nicene of Kipria and Isaakio Anorias is born in Kipryinakros.
 - 14th: County of Cius secedes from the Imperium.
 - 22nd: County of Emisos secedes from the Imperium.
====June
 - 15th: A ceasefire is signed between Agion, allies, and the Imperium. Later extended to a formal peace treaty in which neither side recognizes the claims of the other but agree to end hostilities.
====August
 - Future emperor Hafnio is born to Ioan Niras and Sevasta Fociana Carita in Myrnea.
===645
 - An Agionic army sacks most of the Messene plain with the help of a large horde of northern barbarians over the course of the year. Hundreds of thousands of northerners (mostly Herulenes) are converted and federated into Northern Agion. The invasion of Messinia does little other than bolster Turso's military reputation and destabilize the Imperial borders, in fact likely slowing the demise of the Amicianii, who regain support among the previously pro-Agion Messenes after the brutality of the Agionic raids, especially against a sedentary population such as the Messenes.
===649
====December
 - The Third Great Bay Council is held in Ramonas, with Kiprian, Amirean, and Akrene signatories joining the growing League of Kelos.
==650s [Feb - Nov 2020]
===650
====June
 - A dispute on the terms of allegiance in the League of Kelos results in a large Agionic naval force blockading Vanaea, with the blockade remaining in effect until the next Great Bay Council in 655.
===655
====May
 - The Fourth Great Bay Council is held in Aveleion, Kipria - the first of them to not be held in Agion.
